Hook connect / disconnect is zone-driven on the Belt: entering the danger zone enables auto-connect, and leaving it releases every slot so the hooks can drop into their BLE-idle deep-sleep cycle. The pill shows how Belt is currently hearing the hook: connected = active GATT subscription with status notifies within the last 3 s; advertisement = no live notify but Belt's scan still sees the hook on air; disappear = nothing heard from this hook in either channel for > 5 s.
